Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our AI image generator

What is AI text-to-image generation?

AI text-to-image generation is a process that uses artificial intelligence technology to convert text descriptions into visual images. You only need to input a detailed text description (prompt), and the AI system will generate corresponding images based on your description. This technology combines the latest advances in natural language processing and computer vision.

How do I write good prompts?

Writing good prompts is key to getting the images you want. Here are some tips:

  • Describe the image content in as much detail as possible
  • Specify particular styles like "oil painting", "cyberpunk", "photorealistic", etc.
  • Describe lighting conditions, such as "sunset", "neon lighting"
  • Mention color preferences, like "predominantly blue and purple"
  • Specify image quality, such as "high detail", "4K resolution"

How long does it take to generate an image?

Generating an image typically takes 3-10 seconds, depending on server load and image complexity. During the generation process, you can see a real-time progress bar showing completion status.

What can I use the generated images for?

You can use the generated images for personal and commercial purposes, including:

  • Social media content
  • Blog and website illustrations
  • Creative projects and artwork
  • Product design and marketing materials

However, please note that you should comply with relevant laws and regulations, and not generate or use content that may infringe on the rights of others.

How can I improve the quality of generated images?

Ways to improve the quality of generated images:

  • Use more detailed and specific prompts
  • Clearly specify the artistic style and quality you want
  • Add keywords like "high definition", "rich in detail", "4K resolution"
  • Describe lighting conditions and atmosphere
  • If the results are not ideal, try adjusting the prompt and regenerating
