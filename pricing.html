Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our pricing plans

How do I choose the right plan for me?

When choosing a plan, consider the following factors:

  • How many images you need to generate monthly
  • Your requirements for image quality and resolution
  • Whether you need advanced generation models and editing tools
  • If you need API access for system integration

Individual creators typically start with the Basic plan, professional designers choose the Pro plan, while businesses tend to prefer the Premium plan or Enterprise solutions. You can always start with a lower tier plan and upgrade as your needs grow.

How does billing work?

We offer two billing options:

  • Monthly: Auto-renews each month, can be canceled anytime
  • Annual: One-time payment for the entire year, approximately 16-17% discount

You can start using the service immediately after payment. If you upgrade mid-month, we'll prorate the difference for the remaining days.

Do unused image credits roll over to the next month?

Basic and Pro plans do not roll over unused image credits to the next month. Premium plan allows rolling over unused credits, but only up to one month's worth of credits can be accumulated. Enterprise custom plans can negotiate special rollover rules based on needs.

How can I change or cancel my subscription?

You can change or cancel your subscription anytime from your account settings:

  • Log in to your account
  • Go to "Subscription Management"
  • Select "Change Plan" or "Cancel Subscription"

For monthly subscriptions, you can still use the service until the end of your current billing cycle after cancellation. For annual subscriptions, you can request a prorated refund for unused months, minus the annual discount you received.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial where you can generate up to 10 images to experience our basic features. After the trial period, you can choose to upgrade to a paid plan to continue using the service, or take no action (no automatic charges).
